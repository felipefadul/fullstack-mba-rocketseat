Meet the app

The perfect app for learning to sing

Our Artificial Intelligence algorithm allows us to remove the original voice and keep the melodies of the songs. This way, we have the largest musical repertoire for Karaoke! In addition, as you sing, your voice and pitch are compared by the algorithm, allowing an accurate assessment of your performance and helping you to improve your singing.

The largest Karaoke music library!

Our AI removes the voice from any song and makes it ready to sing, like magic!

Gamified experience

Your performance is scored according to our ultra-precise algorithm. Challenge yourself, earn recognition and celebrate your progress!

Record your performances

Whether you want to share with friends or track your progress, our audio and video recording feature lets you make the most of your experience!

Share with the community

Share your recordings with the community, interact and explore other people's vocal performances on your feed!
