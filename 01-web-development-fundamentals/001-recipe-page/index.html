Coffee cupcake with whipped cream

The perfect muffin to accompany your daily coffee. It's very easy to make and it looks wonderful, it's sure to impress. Make it and let me know what you think!

Time: 1h10
Yield: 12 servings
Difficulty: easy

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups (tea) of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon (soup) of instant coffee
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of oil
  • 2 cups (tea) of sugar
  • 2/3 cup (tea) of cocoa powder
  • 2 and 1/2 cups (tea) of wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon (soup) of baking powder
  • 2 cups (tea) of ready-made whipped cream
  • Coffee beans for decoration
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon (soup) of instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoon (soup) of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons (soup) of butter

Preparation method:

In a blender, beat the water, instant coffee, eggs, oil, sugar and chocolate powder until smooth. Transfer to a bowl, add the flour and baking powder and mix with a spoon. Pour into cupcake liners lined with paper liners and place in a large baking dish, one next to the other. Bake in a preheated medium oven for 25 minutes or until golden brown. Remove and let cool.

For the filling, heat all the ingredients over medium heat, stirring well until thickened. Turn off the heat, let cool and place in a piping bag with a pearl tip. Set aside. Place the whipped cream in a piping bag with a star tip and set aside.

To assemble, make a hole in the center of the cupcakes and fill with the reserved coffee brigadeiro. Decorate the surfaces of the cupcakes with the reserved whipped cream, making circles. Decorate with coffee beans and refrigerate until serving.
